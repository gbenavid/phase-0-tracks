Hello, World.

Many people ask me why I love cats, well... I can explain.

It all started when my two front teeth fell out at the age of six. My Nanny was watching over me for the night and I went running over to her to deliver the exciting news.

The next morning I woke up to find not only money, but also A KITTEN!!!

There he was, waiting for me inside a bubble-gum pink Barbie gift bag.

'What should I name him?!?!' My Nanny helped me come up with a list.

It included:

  • Licorice
  • Slinky
  • Maxi
  • Bamboo
  • Syd

After much contemplation we boiled it down to our top three picks.
