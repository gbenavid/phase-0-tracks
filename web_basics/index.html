Hello, World.

Many people ask me why I love cats, well... I can explain.

It all started when my two front teeth fell out at the age of six. My Nanny was watching over me for the night and I went running over to her to deliver the exciting news.

The next morning I woke up to find a little black & white kitten with a message that read,

'--- Love, The ToothFairy.'

There he was, waiting for me inside a bubble-gum pink Barbie gift bag.

'What should I name him?!?!'

My Nanny and I came up with a list.

It included:

  • Licorice
  • Slinky
  • Maxi
  • Bamboo
  • Syd

After much contemplation we boiled it down to our top three picks.

  1. Maxi
  2. Slinky
  3. Syd

Maxi it is! We cuddled up and I loved him instantly.

Since then I've had a few more cats, each special in their own way. One thing that I appreciate about cats is their shared love for Rachel Maddow. Together, my cats and I have cuddled up every Friday and devoutly tuned in at 9PM (PST)
